The Great Pumpkin Classic Road Race Goes Retro!

You're invited to the 22nd edition of one of the best known races in CT. This year we are going 'Retro', and returning to our roots. Unless you ran in 1991 & '92 most runners don't know that the original Great Pumpkin Classic 4 miler started off as a 5k. We're returning to 3.1 miles which, besides making it shorter, will mean a few less hills & and even more family friendly.

The reason for a change to four miles (way back in 1993) was two-fold. One it was a unique distance and lead to an easily designed course. And by going the extra distance the Great Pumpkin Classic was able to host USATF State Championships for ten years in a row. This attracted some of the fastest runners from CT & beyond to compete for prize money & awards. But now it's 'Back to the Future' for the Great Pumpkin Classic!

Over the years, The Great Pumpkin Classic has donated over $150,000 to the Trumbull High Scholarship Fund to students in financial need. Since its start in 1991 the race has attracted runners from over 85 towns in CT, more than 15 states, and has had winners from five different countries. American running legend Bill Rogers has been here twice along with American Olympic runners & qualifiers. It has also been the site for USATF State Championships for eight years.

New England Runner magazine rated the GPC 'one of the top 50 races in New England' for ten years in a row. The picture that was used for Bill Rogers' shoe sponsor poster was taken on our Great Pumpkin course and used nationally for two years. We thought that with this much history it was time to go retro!

The new course is in the process of development and the start /finish area will depend on the construction and renovations that are concluding at Trumbull High. We'll post a link as soon as we get more information but rest assured we'll have a great run thru the neighborhood and there will be a lot of fun & food waiting for you at the end!

Tell your friends, bring your family (we even have baby sitters!), for the 5k run, the 2 mile walk, and the many kids fun runs on our track. And of course we will always welcome more volunteers & sponsors!

Any questions on the course or distance change contact race founder Marty Schaivone- msrunningproductions@yahoo.com. A link to the tentative course map will be posted as soon as construction & driveway changes are confirmed at the school.
